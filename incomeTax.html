Some Categories and the corresponding forms is crucial for taxpayers to fulfill their filing obligations accurately and comply with tax regulations.

Individual Taxpayers

Includes salaried individuals, freelancers, and professionals earning income independently. They typically use Form ITR-1 (Sahaj) or other relevant forms based on their income sources.

Businesses

Encompasses various entities like companies, partnerships, and LLPs. They may use forms such as ITR-6 (for companies) or other forms based on their structure and income sources.

Non-Resident Indian (NRI) Taxpayers

Individuals living abroad but earning income in India may have specific filing requirements using forms like ITR-2 or ITR-3.

Hindu Undivided Family (HUF)

Treated as separate entities for tax purposes, HUFs may use Form ITR-2 or other applicable forms.

Trusts and Other Entities

Entities like charitable trusts or educational institutions may have unique filing requirements based on their activities and income sources.
