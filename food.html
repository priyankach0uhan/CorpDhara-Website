What is a FSSAI Food License Certificate?

The FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) Food License Certificate is a document issued by the FSSAI to food businesses operating in India. It signifies that the business has complied with the regulations and standards set by the FSSAI regarding food safety and hygiene.

What are the Benefits of Obtaining an FSSAI License?

Consumer Trust

Having an FSSAI license assures consumers that your food business complies with safety standards set by the government, building trust and credibility.

Legal Compliance

It keeps you on the right side of the law, helping you avoid fines, penalties, or even closure due to non-compliance with food safety regulations.

Market Expansion

With an FSSAI license, you gain access to a wider market as many distributors, retailers, and even online platforms require it to sell food products.

Quality Assurance

It ensures that the food you produce meets certain quality standards, giving your customers confidence in the safety and integrity of your products.

Business Reputation

Holding an FSSAI license enhances your business reputation, showing that you prioritize consumer health and safety, which can attract more customers and partners.

Remember : In essence, having an FSSAI license not only keeps your business legal but also helps it thrive by instilling confidence in consumers and opening up new opportunities in the market.

Types of FSSAI licenses

FSSAI Registration

For small-scale food businesses with an annual turnover below a certain threshold.

State FSSAI License

For medium-sized food businesses operating within a single state.

Central FSSAI License

For large-scale food businesses operating in more than one state.
